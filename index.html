UI/UX design is an essential component of developing digital goods such as software applications, mobile apps, and websites. The goal of UI/UX design is to make products easier to use, more accessible, and more satisfying for users overall.

Internet Of Things (IoT)

The term "Internet of Things" (IoT) refers to the interconnectivity of physical objects, buildings, cars (also called "smart devices"), and other items embedded with software, electronics, sensors, actuators, and network connectivity that allow these objects to exchange and collect data.
